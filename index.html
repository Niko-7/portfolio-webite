My Story So Far

  • Ever since i was a small boy i can remember myself following my grandma around in the kitchen trying to understand what she was doing. As soon as i hit 18 i spent two years in culinary school and started working in different restaurants . After years of burns,scrapes and cuts i realized that i only loved cooking as a hobby.

  • I was always a tech savvy and whenever i wasn't cooking i would spend my free time on my computer and so through a friend of mine i got introduced to coding, i can say it was love in the first sight! I Would spend hours online learning to code , its amazing how you can spend hours working on a project and it only feels like a few minutes passed, coding has the power to pull you in and make you forget about everything else!

  • From Chef To Dev

    Soon after i started coding i decided to take a leap of faith , say goodbye to my old life and joined the software development course at the award winning Northcoders bootcamp. It can be very challenging at times but there is nothing more exciting and rewarding than solving a coding problem! The possibilities for learning an improving are endless and im glad i joined Northcoders because through their intense coding bootcamp im one step closer to achieving my goals.

  • With only a few weeks left on the course , im actively looking for a suitable position within your organization in which i can give my best and maximize my potential in order to benefit your organization
